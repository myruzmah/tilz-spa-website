Meet Tilda

Oge Matilda — visionary, entrepreneur, and the heart behind Tilz Spa by Tilda. A sanctuary she built from belief.

Tilda's journey began with a simple conviction: that every woman — and every man — deserves a space where they can exhale. Not just a place for beauty treatments, but a true sanctuary. A place where the noise of Abuja fades, and stillness takes over.

With years of experience in luxury hospitality and a deep passion for wellness, Tilda founded Tilz Spa by Tilda in Wuse 2, Abuja — bringing together world-class spa treatments, private sauna experiences, and executive grooming under one refined roof.

Her philosophy is simple: "Self-care is not indulgence. It is restoration." Every service, every product, every detail at Tilz Spa reflects this belief — minimal luxury, maximum impact.
