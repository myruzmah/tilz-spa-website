Our Story

Where Beauty
Meets Stillness

Tilz Spa by Tilda was born from a simple belief: that self-care is not indulgence — it is restoration. In the heart of Wuse 2, Abuja, we have created a space where sophistication meets serenity, where every detail is intentional, and where you are the priority.

Our approach is minimal luxury — no excess, no noise. Just refined treatments delivered with precision, warmth, and an unwavering commitment to your comfort.

Whether you are seeking a moment of calm between meetings or preparing for life's most important occasions, Tilz Spa is your sanctuary.
