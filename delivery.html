Your Next Steps

Four things to do this week. The rest runs on the rails HAMZURY has built.

1

Log in to the Founder Dashboard

Open login.html with founder@tilzspa.com / TilzSpa@2026. Walk through Calendar, Roadmap, Clients, Staff, and the Social Media plan.

2

Connect Instagram to Canva Content Planner

Open Canva → Content Planner → Connect social. Sign in to the Tilzspa Instagram Business account once. From then on, every design can be scheduled directly.

3

Schedule the first week of posts

Open the Statement template, duplicate, paste Day 1’s caption from the content plan, schedule 8 PM. Repeat for Days 2–7 in one sitting.

4

Print the collateral

Export the Letterhead, Compliment Card, Staff ID and Banner as PDFs from Canva and send them to your preferred printer. Recommend thick cream card stock (300gsm) for the compliment card.
