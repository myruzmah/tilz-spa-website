Integration

Setup Guide

A seven-step walkthrough for the Meta Business WhatsApp API.

✓

Step 1 · Create Meta Business Account

Go to business.facebook.com and create or link a business account for Tilzspar.

✓

Step 2 · Create a Meta App

Visit developers.facebook.com, choose Create App, select “Business” type, name it “Tilzspar”.

3

Step 3 · Add WhatsApp Product

In the Meta App dashboard, click Add Product, choose WhatsApp, and follow the setup wizard.

4

Step 4 · Verify Business Number

Add +234 916 769 8000 as the WhatsApp Business number and complete OTP verification.

5

Step 5 · Configure Webhook

Set a webhook URL to receive incoming messages. Requires a server endpoint — contact HAMZURY to set this up.

6

Step 6 · Submit Message Templates

Submit the templates in the Templates tab to Meta for review. Approval takes 24–48 hours.

7

Step 7 · Go Live

Once templates are approved and the webhook is live, enable automation rules in the Rules tab.
