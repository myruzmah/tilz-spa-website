Setup Guide

7-step Meta Business API walkthrough

✓

Step 1 — Create Meta Business Account

Go to business.facebook.com and create or link your business account for Tilz Spa.

✓

Step 2 — Create a Meta App

Visit developers.facebook.com → Create App → Select "Business" type → Name it "Tilz Spa".

3

Step 3 — Add WhatsApp Product

In your Meta App dashboard, click "Add Product" → Select "WhatsApp" → Follow the setup wizard.

4

Step 4 — Verify Business Phone Number

Add +234 916 769 8000 as your WhatsApp Business number and complete OTP verification.

5

Step 5 — Configure Webhook

Set webhook URL to receive incoming messages. Requires a server endpoint — contact HAMZURY to set this up.

6

Step 6 — Submit Message Templates

Submit your approved message templates (see Templates tab) to Meta for review. Approval takes 24–48 hours.

7

Step 7 — Go Live

Once templates are approved and webhook is live, enable automation rules in the Automation tab.
